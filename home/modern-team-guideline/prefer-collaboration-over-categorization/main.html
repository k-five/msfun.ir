prefer-collaboration-over-categorization

We usually tend - and it has been in the past - to organize or group people by ranking them from top to button. A hierarchy of values or positions or skills.

In fact we are categorizing.

  • one CTO
  • two back-ends
  • three front-ends
  • etc

Pretty common model all over world.

But in the microservices world that our focus should be on values and products categorizing people will lead us to have incidental coupled team.

collaboration

With microservices when we make team around bounded context the thing that becomes important is collaboration and not categorization.

The more collaborative a team is, the fast and easy they can solve their challenges.

Also people tend to take more responsibilities in a collaborative environment then a categorized environment.
