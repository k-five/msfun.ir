Continuous Delivery is another step we add to our pipeline in which we deliver our production code to the end-users.

This step is a Business Decision and not Developers Decision.

We may have some features at production level ready for end-users, but we do not want to show / deliver them to the end-users.

That is it. Lets move to CI Server.
