If you open any online articles related to pipeline and microservices in which they want to talk about how a pipeline works? or watch youtube.com free videos or pay to watch premium videos they jump directory to Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery plus Continuous Development.

They say how a pipeline works but they do not say based on what facts this pipeline works?

The two key concepts which are missed are:

  1. Kernel Notification
  2. File-System Observation

In fact talking about pipeline without talking about those two is like talking about the Internet without talking about TCP: Transfer Control Protocol.

Thus not like others we first learn about Kernel Notification and File-System Observation and what they do. Then based on capabilities that these two give us we can create our pipeline.
